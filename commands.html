Introduction

Levox music is a music bot on Discord that allows users to easily play , pause and skip songs via text commands within voice channels , providing a fun music experience on servers.

About this Bot

Name : Levox Music

Date Of Creation : 12/08/2024

Prefix : L!

Devloped By : Levox Team

Technology

The bot is bulit with javascript using the discord.js library, same with the API the bot uses.

This bot was programmed by Levox Team . They used many programming languages ​​such as JavaScript , Jason and some other languages . This bot was completed in just two days because its developers are very fast .

Getting Started

To get started with our bot is fairly easy and we have done our best to make it simpler for you.

In-bot help

There is also a in-bot gettingstarted command that you can use if you know the installation process.
You also have the option to get help on all the bot commands right through discord with the use of the bot's internal help command.

Important: You must agree to our imagepolicy and matter regarding it will be fairly strict.
Use L!help for more information, or join in server support and send your problem or question.

Adding the bot

To add the bot to your server click here.

To get started , click the invite button in the main menu. And enter the bot into your server .
